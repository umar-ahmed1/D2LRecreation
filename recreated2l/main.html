Old Zoom recordings to be deleted Aug. 1, 2022

Posted Nov 7, 2022 9:00 AM

Starting on Aug. 1, 2022, all Zoom cloud recordings that are older than two years will be moved to your Zoom trash for 30 days, during which time Zoom will send email reminders explaining how to delete or download this content before it is permanently deleted. Ensure you don't lose your videos by downloading them to your computer or a UCalgary server and/or migrating them to the online video platform YuJa.
